Provins Day Trip – France Trip 2025

Visited March 30, 2025

Exploring Medieval France

We took a short trip from Paris to Provins, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its medieval architecture and underground galleries.

Highlights

  • Guided tour through the underground tunnels used by merchants and knights
  • Live medieval horse show with knights, jousting, and falconry
  • Walking the old town walls and enjoying crepes at a local café
