France Family Trip – March 2025

Started: March 26, 2025

A Family Adventure Begins

Our family trip to France began on March 26 with an overnight flight from RDU to London Heathrow. We landed in London on the morning of March 27, then boarded a short connecting flight to Paris.

We’re spending the first part of our trip in Serris, a beautiful suburb just 1.6 km from Disney Paris. The condo we’re renting is comfortable and close enough to walk or shuttle to the parks.

Disney Paris

We spent Friday and Saturday enjoying all that Disney Paris has to offer. From classic rides to meeting characters, the experience has been magical. Stay tuned for a dedicated page with photos and highlights from our Disney days.

Provins, France

On Sunday, we took a day trip to Provins, a charming medieval town. We toured the underground galleries and caught a spectacular horse show that brought the town’s history to life. There’s much more to share from this adventure too.
