Welcome to My Cooking Journey

I love to cook and typically prepare meals for my family four to five times a week. Most of the time, these are simple and delicious meals, but I also enjoy trying new recipes and experimenting with dishes I've never made before.

When it comes to grilling, I fire up my Weber 22-inch Kettle and Weber Spirit E310 gas grill, and in the past couple of years, I've really gotten into smoking meats on the Weber. Whether it's brisket, ribs, or pulled pork, I enjoy the process of low-and-slow cooking.

Inside the kitchen, we have Taco Tuesdays and Pasta Thursdays, which have become family traditions. From classic comfort foods to new cooking adventures, this section of the site is where I'll share recipes, techniques, and what's sizzling in my kitchen and on the grill.

Stay tuned for BBQ, smoked meats, family meals, and cocktail pairings!
